Package Overview

Embark on a spiritually uplifting journey with our Premium Comfort Umroh package. Designed for pilgrims who seek a balance of luxury and value, this package ensures you stay just steps away from the Holy Mosques while enjoying top-tier services.

From direct flights via Saudia Airlines to 5-star accommodations with full-board meals, every detail is meticulously planned. Our experienced Mutawwif will guide you through the rituals, ensuring your worship is performed correctly and with peace of mind.

Itinerary Schedule

Day 1: Departure

Jakarta - Jeddah - Makkah

Gather at Soekarno-Hatta Airport for briefing and departure. Arrival in Jeddah, proceed to Makkah by private bus. Perform first Umrah.

Day 2-5: Ibadah in Makkah

Makkah Al-Mukarramah

Daily prayers at Masjid Al-Haram. Optional second Umrah. Ziarah around Makkah (Jabal Thur, Jabal Rahmah, Mina, Arafat, Muzdalifah).

Day 6: Transfer to Madinah

Makkah - Madinah (Haramain Train)

Experience the high-speed Haramain Train to Madinah (approx 2.5 hours). Check in hotel. Visit Raudhah (scheduled).

Day 7-11: Ibadah in Madinah

Madinah Al-Munawwarah

Daily prayers at Masjid Nabawi. Ziarah Madinah (Masjid Quba, Date Farm, Uhud Mountain). Religious lectures by Ustadz.

Day 12: Return

Madinah - Jakarta

Check out and transfer to Prince Mohammad bin Abdulaziz Airport. Flight back to Jakarta. Arrival and end of services.
